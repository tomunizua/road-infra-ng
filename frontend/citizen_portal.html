About RoadWatch Nigeria

Empowering citizens to improve Nigerian infrastructure through AI-powered community reporting and data-driven decision making.

Our Mission

We're transforming how Nigeria manages road infrastructure by creating a transparent, data-driven system that connects citizens, governments, and maintenance teams.

Every report submitted helps build a comprehensive database that informs better maintenance planning and budget allocation for safer, more reliable roads.

Our Key Goals

Early Detection

Reduce road damage through early detection and swift response

Budget Optimization

Optimize maintenance budgets with data-driven allocation

Data-Driven Decisions

Enable informed decision making through comprehensive analytics

Community Engagement

Engage communities in improving Nigeria's infrastructure
